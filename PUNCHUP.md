1. What is the work you’re taking on?
I plan to create a small mobile app as a coding project for my portfolio. The app will allow users to keep a record of groceries in their fridge, freezer, and pantry by adding items and setting their expiration dates.

2. How do you think this is going to enhance your body of work?
I currently don’t have a portfolio piece that demonstrates my development skills. This project will serve as a new entry and a small, self-initiated project that highlights my ability to design and build a functional app.

3. Any resources, fears, uncertainties, or doubts?
The idea is based on my own needs and includes fairly simple features, so I’m concerned that viewers might see it as unnecessary or uninteresting. From a technical standpoint, I also feel uncertain about the implementation, as I lack confidence in my coding skills.
